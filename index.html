We are a group of individuals, friends,families, construction workers, businessmen, graphic designers, and entrepeneurs who have made it our life goal to alleviate poverty and promote prosperity within the community of Philadelphia, Pennsylvannia.

Prosperity is an organization focused on lowering the numbers of poverty in America. We help people break the cycle of poverty with education, job placement, and home assistance. Prosperity is a non-profit that is run by generous donations from people like you and the sacrifice of volunteers.

Reasons for homelessness include: competitive living wages, lack of public support and benefits, lack of affordable transportation, lack of inexpensive housing, lack of health care, and etc. There are many reasons for homelessness. So are solutions to solve it.
